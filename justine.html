PayPark - Prototype

December 2016
PayPark is a prototype for an app that allows you to find parking nearby, pay for parking right from your phone, and find stores or events near you. It's difficult to find parking in major cities like San Francisco, Los Angeles, and New York. However, with PayPark, people will have an easier time finding available parking nearby and be able to pay straight through their phone. I was responsible for the homepage, logins, and QR code designs. For more information, please check out the link to a powerpoint presentation and the PDF that has exact images of the prototype.
